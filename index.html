Featured project

Status: In progress / experimental

SmartHub — Local AI Assistant Hub

SmartHub is my experimental local AI assistant project. I am building it as a modular hub that can connect local models with real computer actions, starting with browser control, YouTube search, simple task history, and app automation.

The goal is to learn how local AI can actually help on a personal computer without relying only on cloud tools or stopping at chatbot replies. I am experimenting with local models through LM Studio, then connecting those responses to practical actions through tools like Python, FastAPI/Uvicorn, Playwright, and PyAutoGUI.

I am also exploring how SmartHub could connect with my NAS/home lab setup, so the assistant can eventually be reached from devices at home instead of being tied to one machine.

Android AR prototype

Wall Scanner 3D Pro

A work-in-progress Android AR prototype for wall scanning and basic 3D room reconstruction. The goal is to detect walls and common wall elements, then create exportable models that could help with renovation planning or room visualization.

The project is still evolving, especially around recognition accuracy and full-room completion. It has been a good way to explore ARCore, computer vision ideas, and the limits of on-device processing.

  • Kotlin
  • ARCore
  • OpenCV
  • 3D modeling concepts

Home Lab / NAS Setup

Storage, backups, and local access at home.

My NAS/home lab setup is where I experiment with storage, backups, file sharing, and local network access. I use it to keep files organized, practice syncing and backup workflows, and learn how devices talk to each other on a home network.

I am also exploring how it can connect with my SmartHub project, so a local AI assistant can be accessed around the house instead of being tied to one machine. This is still in-progress, but it is helping me learn practical networking, storage, and self-hosting in a hands-on way.
